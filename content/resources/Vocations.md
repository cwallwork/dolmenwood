## __Hunter__
You character may start with a simple animal companion (dog, cat, hawk, snake, weasel etc.) or take one automatic success on establishing a connection with a wild animal during play.

Animal companions understand simple commands, and will fight to defend the hunter if threatened.
### d8 HP

#### __Starting equipment__
__Armour__ (roll 1d6):

4–6 Leather armour + shield.
