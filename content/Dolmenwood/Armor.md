### Light Armor
e.g. Leather
1 armor

### Medium Armor
e.g. Chain mail
2 armor, 2 Slots

### Heavy Armor
e.g. Plate mail
3 armor, 2 Slots, prevents dodging

### Shields
Enable blocking, and can be sacrificed to ignore an incoming
attack.

### Helmets
Can negate injuries from failed Critical Damage Saves
