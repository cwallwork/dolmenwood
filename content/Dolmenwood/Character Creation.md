# Roll ability scores

Roll 3d6 three times. Assign each total to one of the ability scores of your choice, STR, DEX and WIl. Enter the numbers in the 'MAX' box.

Roll 1d4 and add 10 to get LUCK

## Add Modifiers
For each of the three stats, enter the modifier in the MOD column. The modifier is the (stat - 10) /2
For each stat this will give you a modifier between -4 and +4.
## Choose a Vocation

Choose one of the [[Vocations]] for your character: Cleric, Hunter, Knight, Magician or Thief.

### Weapons and Armor
Roll for armor and weapons using the list in your vocation description. Write them in the "On person" section of your character sheet. Most equipment occupies one slot (box) on the character sheet. Some equipment takes more than one slot. Some equipment takes no slots. Anything that takes 0 slots goes in the 'Skills, Spells, etc.' section.
### Equipment, Skills and Spells
Add the equipment listed in your vocation to either the "on person" section or to your backpack. During combat it takes one action to move an item (or swap two items) from in your backpack to on your person.
Add any class skills or spells 
### Hit Protection (HP)
Roll the listed die type for your vocation, put it in the MAX Hp box
## Motivation
Pick which of these motivations best suits your character:
- Helping people
- Uncovering mysteries
- Seeking treasure
