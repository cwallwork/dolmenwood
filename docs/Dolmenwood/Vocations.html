Cleric

For this campaign your character will start as a cleric of the Pluritine Church. Your character has yet to earn the right to join one of the holy orders.

d6HP

Starting equipment

Armour: (roll 1d6): 1 Leather. 2 Leather + shield. 3 Chainmail(2 Slots). 4 Chainmail(2 Slots) + shield. 5 Plate mail(2 Slots). 6 Plate mail(2 Slots) + shield.

Weapons: (roll 1d6 twice): 1 Dagger. 2 Longsword. 3 Mace. 4 Shortbow + 20 arrows. 5 Shortsword. 6 Warhammer (2 Slots).

Class items: Wooden holy symbol

Basic Items Rations (3 uses) Torches (3 uses) Bandages (3 uses) tinder box (0 slots)

Skills

Detect holy magic Turn undead

Magic

A rank 1 holy spell (choose one or roll 1d8)

1 Detect Evil 2 Detect Magic 3 Frost Ward 4 Lesser Healing 5 Light 6 Mantle of Protection 7 Purify Food and Drink 8 Rally

Hunter

You character may start with a simple animal companion (dog, cat, hawk, snake, weasel etc.) or take one automatic success on establishing a connection with a wild animal during play.

Animal companions understand simple commands, and will fight to defend the hunter if threatened.

d8 HP

Starting equipment

Armour (roll 1d6): 1–3 Leather armour. 4–6 Leather armour + shield.

Weapons (roll 1d6 twice): 1 Dagger. 2 Longsword. 3–4 Longbow(2 Slots) + 20 arrows. 5 Shortsword 6 Sling + 20 stones.

Basic Equipment Firewood(2 Slots, 6 hours) Tent large trap tinder box (0 slots)

Skills

Animal Companion Hunting skills (tracking, navigating etc.)

Knight

For this campaign your character will start as a squire of house Harrowmoor. Your character has yet to earn their knighthood.

d8HP

Starting equipment

Armour (roll 1d6): 1 Chainmail 2–3 Chainmail + shield 4 Plate Mail(2 Slots) 5–6 Plate Mail (2 Slots) + shield.

Weapons (roll 1d6 twice): 1 Dagger 2–4 Lance 5 Longsword 6 Mace

Basic Equipment 3d6 Gold Pieces Rations (3 uses) Torches (3 uses) Bandages (3 uses) tinder box (0 slots)

Skills Horsemanship Hospitality (may request food and shelter from any of the noble houses)
